Detailed Description

This PPI provides services to register a platform specific notification callback for ResetSystem(). The registered handlers are processed after EDKII_PLATFORM_SPECIFIC_RESET_FILTER_PPI notifications and before EDKII_PLATFORM_SPECIFIC_RESET_HANDLER_PPI notifications.
