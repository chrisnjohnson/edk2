[Bits 27:24] TCC Activation Offset (R/W) Specifies a temperature offset in degrees C from the temperature target (bits 23:16). PROCHOT# will assert at the offset target temperature. Write is permitted only MSR_PLATFORM_INFO.[30] is set.
