Detailed Description

PCD_CRYPTO_SERVICE_FAMILY_ENABLE structure. Each field in this structure is associated with a service in the EDK II Crypto Protocol/PPI. This allows each individual service to be enabled/disabled in a DSC file. Services are also grouped into families. Unions are used to support enabling or disabling an entire family in a single DSC statement.
